The Ubiquitous Power of Calculus

How Calculus Shapes Every STEM Discipline

Key Points

Understanding the Language of Change

  • Calculus is the mathematical study of continuous change.
  • Differential Calculus: Focuses on rates of change and slopes of curves (e.g., velocity, acceleration).
  • Integral Calculus: Focuses on accumulation of quantities and areas under curves (e.g., total distance, volume).
  • Provides tools to model, analyze, and predict dynamic systems.
Key Points

Building the Future: Calculus in Engineering

  • Civil Engineering: Structural analysis, fluid dynamics, stress distribution.
  • Mechanical Engineering: Kinematics, dynamics, thermodynamics, material science.
  • Electrical Engineering: Circuit analysis (AC/DC), signal processing, electromagnetic fields.
  • Aerospace Engineering: Rocket propulsion, aerodynamics, orbital mechanics.

Unveiling the Universe: Physics & Astronomy
